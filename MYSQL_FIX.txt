=============================================
  MYSQL max_allowed_packet FIX FOR XAMPP
=============================================

Problem: "Got a packet bigger than 'max_allowed_packet' bytes"

SOLUTION:

Step 1: Open my.ini file
  Location: C:\xampp\mysql\bin\my.ini

Step 2: Find [mysqld] section

Step 3: Add this line under [mysqld]:
  max_allowed_packet=64M

Step 4: Save and restart MySQL from XAMPP

Done!
